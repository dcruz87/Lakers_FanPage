Lakers Fun Facts

Fun Fact #1

The LA Lakers started as the Minneapolis Lakers. The team name references Minnesota’s nickname, “Land of 10,000 Lakes.”

Fun Fact #2

The Lakers have more fans on Facebook than any other U.S. sports team, and the most Twitter followers of all NBA teams.

Fun Fact #3

The team has won 16 titles in all—the second highest number in the NBA.
